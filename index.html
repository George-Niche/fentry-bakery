    About Fentry

    Fentry is a bakery that specializes in producing delicious baked goods using high-quality ingredients. We provide a selection of pastries, cookies, and savory treats, perfect for on-the-go snacking or an indulgent midday break.

    Whether you're looking for a fresh, crusty baguette to accompany dinner, a rich and moist cake for a special occasion, or a simple yet satisfying pastry to enjoy with your morning coffee, Fentry offers something for everyone. Come visit us and experience the warmth, craftsmanship, and quality that has made us a staple in the community for over a century.
